Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What documents do I need to apply? ▾
    You'll need a government-issued ID, recent pay stubs (last 2–3 months), proof of income, and your employer's contact information. You can upload all of these directly in the application form below. We may also ask for references from previous landlords.
  • All your properties are rented — why should I still apply? ▾
    Submitting an application puts you on our waitlist. When a unit becomes available that matches your needs and budget, we reach out to pre-approved applicants first. Getting approved now means you won't miss out when a home opens up.
  • How long does the approval process take? ▾
    We aim to respond within 24 business hours of receiving a completed application with all required documents. If we need additional information, we'll reach out by phone or email right away.
  • Are pets allowed? ▾
    Pet policies vary by property. Several of our single-family homes are pet-friendly with a pet deposit. Please mention your pets in the application notes, including type and breed, and we'll match you with suitable listings.
  • What income is required to qualify? ▾
    Generally, we look for a monthly gross income of 3× the monthly rent. However, we review each application individually — strong rental history, references, or other factors can also help your application. We're a small company and we take the time to look at the full picture.
  • Is my information kept private? ▾
    Absolutely. All information you submit — including pay stubs and personal documents — is used solely to evaluate your rental application and is kept strictly confidential. We do not share your information with third parties.
